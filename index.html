About

Muñoz Españolita brings the warmth of Spain to your table with a focus on pristine ingredients, classic technique, and contemporary presentation. Settle in for an evening of tapas shared among friends, a leisurely paella, and desserts inspired by the Iberian coast.

  • Seasonal Spanish fare
  • Thoughtful Iberian wines
  • Handcrafted cocktails
